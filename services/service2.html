How It Works

A straightforward process focused on clarity and usability

1

Discovery Call (free, 30 min)

We talk through the metrics that matter to you, where your data lives, and what the dashboard needs to help you decide. No commitment required.

2

Data Connection & Preparation

I connect to your data source (CSV, Excel, Google Sheets, or database), clean it, and structure it so the dashboard stays accurate and up to date.

3

Dashboard Design & Build

I build the dashboard and share a draft for your feedback before finalising. You can request changes to layout, metrics, or filters at this stage.

4

Delivery & Walkthrough

You receive the finished dashboard plus a short walkthrough call covering how to use filters, read the charts, and update the data yourself going forward.
