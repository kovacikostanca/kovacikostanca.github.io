How It Works

A clear, structured process — from problem definition to working solution

1

Discovery Call (free, 30 min)

We define the problem clearly: what you want to predict or automate, what data you have, and what success looks like. I'll tell you honestly if the project is feasible with your current data.

2

Data Audit & Scoping

I review your data, identify gaps or quality issues, and propose a specific approach — with a fixed price and timeline agreed before any work begins.

3

Model Development & Validation

I build, test, and validate the model with appropriate metrics — not just accuracy. I document every decision so you know what trade-offs were made and why.

4

Delivery, Demo & Handoff

You receive deployment-ready code, documentation, and a working demo (Streamlit app or API). We walk through the results together and I explain what the model can and cannot do.
