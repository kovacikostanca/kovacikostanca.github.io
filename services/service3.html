What I Help With

Focused on the technical analysis side — not academic writing

Data Cleaning & Preparation

Survey exports, experimental results, scraped data — I handle messy real-world datasets and return something structured, consistent, and ready to analyse.

Statistical Analysis

Descriptive stats, hypothesis tests (t-tests, ANOVA, chi-square), correlation, regression — applied correctly for your data type, with results explained in plain language.

Visualisations for Reports & Theses

Clean, publication-quality charts and figures in Python (Matplotlib, Seaborn) or Tableau — formatted to match your institution's style requirements.

Reproducible Analysis Notebooks

All work delivered as documented Jupyter notebooks — so your supervisor can follow every step, and you can explain and defend your methodology confidently.

How It Works

A simple, collaborative process aligned with your academic deadlines

1

Initial Discussion (free, 30 min)

We go through your research questions, your dataset, and what your supervisor or assignment requires. I'll tell you honestly what I can support and what's outside my scope.

2

Scope, Timeline & Fixed Price

I outline exactly what I'll deliver, by when, and at what cost — agreed upfront. Academic deadlines are real; I take them seriously.

3

Analysis & Documentation

I run the analysis with step-by-step documentation of every method, assumption, and decision — so you can explain your choices to your supervisor or examiner.

4

Delivery & Walkthrough

You receive your cleaned data, results, visualisations, and Jupyter notebook. We go through the findings together so you fully understand — and can discuss — every result.

Important Note on Academic Integrity

I provide technical data analysis support only. I do not write academic text, interpret findings in your voice, or engage in any form of academic misconduct. The analysis is a tool you use — the understanding and the writing are yours.
