About me

Kostanca Kovaci

Hi, I'm Kostanca — a data scientist, available to work remotely with startups and businesses across Europe. My background is a little unusual: I studied Physics before specialising in Data Science, which means I bring both rigorous analytical thinking and the technical depth to build and ship real solutions.

I work across three areas:
• 𝐃𝐚𝐭𝐚 𝐚𝐧𝐚𝐥𝐲𝐬𝐢𝐬 & 𝐫𝐞𝐩𝐨𝐫𝐭𝐢𝐧𝐠 — cleaning messy datasets, statistical analysis, clear findings
• 𝐃𝐚𝐬𝐡𝐛𝐨𝐚𝐫𝐝𝐬 & 𝐯𝐢𝐬𝐮𝐚𝐥𝐢𝐬𝐚𝐭𝐢𝐨𝐧 — interactive tools that help non-technical teams actually use & learn from their data
• 𝐌𝐋 & 𝐍𝐋𝐏 — classification models, text analysis, and research data (my MSc thesis was original research on bias in large language models)

What matters to me is the last mile — the insight that actually gets acted on. Everything I build is not just technically sound, but understood and useful to the people who need it. I also share what I learn through tutorials and writing, because good data thinking should be accessible to everyone.
